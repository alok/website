About Us

General

Nikkei Choral Ensemble, also known as NiCE, is an a cappella group at UC Berkeley that arranges and performs songs in both Japanese and English. We are a group of fun-loving individuals who enjoy sharing good music and making people smile.

Humble Beginnings

Nikkei Choral Ensemble was founded in 2010 by Naomi Amakawa originally as an open student musical group, no audition required, within the Nikkei Student Union (NSU). The group, without an official name, served as more of a social music group rather than a performing arts group and only occasionally performed at cultural events.

From Fall 2011-Spring 2016, Nikkei Choral Ensemble operated as an autonomous group with the NSU. Holding their first showcase in Spring 2012, members of NiCE began to focus on performance as a key component of group activity. The group began auditioning new members within the same semester.

In Fall 2016, Nikkei Choral Ensemble became an independent RSO musical group.

Our Music

Each semester, we aim to put on a public showcase of various pieces we work on as a group. Our entirely member-selected and arranged repertoire consists of a wide variety of in both Japanese and English, ranging from western pop and indie songs to songs from anime and games, Japanese classics, and more.
